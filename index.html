Research

[MSc Thesis] Molecular Dynamics Study of Effect of Plasticizers on Ion Transport in Polyethylene Oxide (PEO)-LiTFSI Electrolytes for Li-ion Batteries

Performed molecular dynamics simulation on SPE (i.e. poly(ethylene oxide)) consisting of LiTFSI salt and SN plasticisers.

Calculated the ion diffusivity and explained the ion transport mechanism using ion-pairing and polymer segmental motion of PEO chains.
